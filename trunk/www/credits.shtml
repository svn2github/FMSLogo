Credits

FMSLogo was originally called MSWLogo. It was written by George Mills and based on UCBLogo, which is written by Brian Harvey and is still under active development. A large, international community adopted MSWLogo, translated it into several languages, and created countless lessons plans around it. Today, FMSLogo has a home on SourceForge. It is my hope that SourceForge will serve as a focal point for future community involvement.
